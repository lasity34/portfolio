Who I am

Designer and developer based in Cape Town

Originally hailing from Cape Town, I embarked on a nine-year journey in the hospitality sector. Starting as a waiter and eventually rising to the position of General Manager, I honed my skills in communication and business management. Leading teams of up to 100 individuals, I gained invaluable experience that was further enriched by my time working in Dubai, where I was exposed to a diverse range of cultures.

In 2015, I took a strategic turn in my career, diving into the world of software development, a field that had always fascinated me. Since making this pivotal change, I have fully committed myself to mastering both front-end and back-end development. On the front-end, I specialize in React, while on the back-end, I have honed my skills in technologies such as Express and Node.js. Beyond development, I also have experience in testing, ensuring the robustness and reliability of my projects. This technical expertise is further augmented by my skills in marketing and social media, making me a well-rounded professional.
